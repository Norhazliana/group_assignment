About Us

Serving Since 2017

Our Story

"Founded in 2017 by pastry maestro Carlo Rossi, 'Pastry Charm' is a sweet haven celebrated for its enchanting Cromboloni—a fusion of croissant and bomboloni—and irresistible Cruffles.

Carlo's culinary journey, inspired by family traditions, has transformed this cozy shop into a local gem. Every bite at 'Pastry Charm' is a symphony of flavors, a testament to Carlo's dedication to infuse pride and passion into each creation. Indulge in the magic, savor the sweetness!"
